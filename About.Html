About Us

Welcome to SoleMates

At SoleMates, we're passionate about bringing you the hottest and rarest sneakers from around the world. Our mission is to provide sneaker enthusiasts with a curated selection of authentic and limited- edition kicks, while delivering exceptional customer service and a seamless online shopping experience.

Our Story

Founded by Alex Chen in 2018, SoleMates was born out of a love for sneakers and a frustration with the lack of accessibility to rare and limited-edition shoes. As sneakerheads ourselves, we know how hard it can be to find the shoes you want, and how disappointing it is to miss out on a release. That's why we've made it our goal to connect sneaker enthusiasts with the shoes they desire, while building a community that shares our passion for sneaker culture.
